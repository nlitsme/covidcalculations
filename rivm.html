Data from rivm. More tables: country info, Dutch municipalities, Worldwide vaccinations, US vaccinations.

This table shows the current Covid-19 infection levels in the various Dutch municipalities and provinces. Click table headers to sort, slide the filter to smooth the graphs, slide 'cutoff' up to turn off smoothing filter, use `mincases` to show less entries. Note that the filtered graphs will be shorter, this can be adjusted with the 'bvalue' slider, this makes the filter less accurate. Click on the graph for a detailed graph view.
